Hello, my name is Justin

I like to think of myself as a pretty honest and unassuming guy, just trying to do the best I can with the time I have. In my day job I help people make computers do things. Sometimes the computers are big, sometimes they’re small, and sometimes they talk. Outside of work I enjoy running, drinking pints of tasty craft beer, and of course spending time with friends and family. I want to spend more time hiking and fishing - trying to fix that.

Michigan raised, and a Midwesterner to the core, but I've called the Pacific Northwest home for a good long while now. At present that means Tacoma, WA. I believe this is the best city the PNW has to offer and if you disagree you're wrong!

Want to get in touch? Go ahead and shoot me an email. I'd love to hear from you!
